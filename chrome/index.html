Small, but complete.

Everything a casual session needs fits into one dropdown — model, prompt, orientation, Generate, last results. The Models tab is where you pull any SD repo from Hugging Face without leaving the popup; download keeps running in the service worker even after you close it.

01 Violet for the verb.Generate, Get — the two moments of commitment get the accent. Everything else is quiet.
02 Get / Token / n/a.Three honest states. A model doesn’t pretend to be installable if your VRAM can’t host it.
03 One download at a time.While anything is streaming, the tab badge shows a small violet dot. Close the popup — it keeps going.
04 Workspace behind ↗.Heavy work (history, tuning, bulk) opens as a full tab. The popup never tries to be it.
